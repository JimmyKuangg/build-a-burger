Build-A-Burger
  • Get patties from the table on the left in the lower right door and put them on the griddle
  • When patties change color on the bottom, it means they are cooked on the bottom. Click them to flip them
  • When patties are fully cooked, take them off the griddle and put them on the buns
  • Drag buns from lower left door in the table on the left and put them on top of the patties
  • Drag hamburgers to the window to score a point
  • Put out as many hamburgers as you can within the time limit!
